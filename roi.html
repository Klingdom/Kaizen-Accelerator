ROI model

Make the math obvious.

We estimate impact using conservative assumptions across labor recovered, rework reduced, and cycle time compression (cash velocity).

Start Intake View Standard

1) Labor efficiency gain

Employees impacted × hours saved/week × loaded hourly cost × 52

Example: 10 employees × 3 hrs/week × $75/hr × 52 = $117,000 annualized impact

2) Defect / rework reduction

(Defect % reduction) × volume × cost/defect × 12

Example: 2,000 tx/mo × (15%→10%) × $50 × 12 = $60,000 annualized savings

3) Cycle time compression (cash velocity)

Translate days removed into accelerated revenue timing and reduced working capital drag.

We quantify this in the engagement using your actual pipeline/onboarding/release data.

4) Leadership escalation drag

Leaders × hours/week saved × loaded rate × 52

Example: 3 leaders × 4 hrs/week × $150/hr × 52 = $93,600
