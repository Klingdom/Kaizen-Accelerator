Start Intake ROI model

Sales‑to‑Cash

Symptoms: approvals, stuck deals, messy CRM stages, invoice delays.

KPIs: cycle time, approval time, rework rate, invoice delay, slip by stage.

30‑day wins: fewer handoffs, standard stages, clean ownership, faster invoicing.

Customer Onboarding

Symptoms: slow go‑live, inconsistent kickoff, customer data gaps, escalations.

KPIs: time‑to‑value, rework %, milestone adherence, early churn indicators.

30‑day wins: readiness checklist, standardized playbook, milestone governance.

Product / Release Flow

Symptoms: unpredictable releases, WIP overload, QA bottlenecks, hotfixes.

KPIs: cycle time, throughput, WIP, hotfix rate, escape defects.

30‑day wins: stage gates, WIP limits, release checklist, stable cadence.
